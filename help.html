Need Help? We’re Here for You

At SaveLink, we understand that finding a donor can be stressful. This guide will help you navigate our platform and get the support you need.

How to Request Help

  1. Sign up or log in to your account
  2. Click on "Request Donation" and enter details
  3. Our system will match you with donors nearby
  4. Contact donors securely through the platform

How to Become a Donor

  1. Register as a donor with health details
  2. Choose group of blood
  3. Get notified when someone needs help
  4. Coordinate with the recipient

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes! We prioritize security and never share your data without consent.

❓ Can I choose who I donate to?

✔ Yes, you have complete control over your donations.

❓ Is SaveLink free?

✔ Absolutely! Our goal is to connect people without any cost.
